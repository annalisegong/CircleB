Volleyball League

Choose from Tuesday, Wednesday or Thursday evenings, or Sunday afternoon’s throughout the Summer.

All of our volleyball leagues are co-ed leagues. We feature 2 regulation sand courts, a full service bar next to the courts and our full restaurant menu is available for delivery out to the volleyball area. Cost is $125 per team for the entire season.

Read more

Horse Shoe League

Choose from Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday Nights throughout the Summer. Horseshoe leagues consist of teams of 2 people.

Play 1 match a night of 3 games of 21 shoes each.

Cost is $50.00 a team for the whole season, and substitutes are allowed, so feel free to have subs.

Read more

Bean Bag League

Our bean bag toss leagues consist of 2 people per team. Playing 3 games to 21 against the same team a week.
Our equipment meet ACO regulations. We will try to form leagues on Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday nights throughout the Summer
We are now offering a Indoor Winter Bean Bag Toss league - approximately 10-12 week season.
